Research

My research involves various topics revolving around the notion of information. It is mainly directed towards understanding the fundamental limits of information transmission and information storage systems, as well as towards constructing efficient communication schemes and error control mechanisms that approach these limits. I am especially interested in problems arising in zero-error information theory (the limits of communication under the stringent requirement of perfect reliability) and algebraic/combinatorial coding theory (properties and constructions of codes with specified error correction capabilities).
