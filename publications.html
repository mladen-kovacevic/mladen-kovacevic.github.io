Research

My research includes various topics in information theory, coding theory, and cryptography. I am generally interested in the mathematical foundations and computational/algorithmic aspects of these and related subjects, as well as in their applications.

All publications listed below can be downloaded from my arXiv or ResearchGate webpages.
Other research profiles:   Google Scholar, ORCiD, Web of Science, Scopus, IEEE, dblp, CSAuthors, zbMATH.
