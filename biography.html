Short Bio

Mladen Kovačević was born in Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina, in 1984. He received the M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in electrical and computer engineering (communications and signal processing) from the University of Novi Sad, Serbia, where he is currently an Associate Professor. From October 2015 to May 2018 he worked as a Research Fellow at the National University of Singapore. His research interests include information theory, coding theory, and cryptography. He is an IEEE Senior Member.
